About DayCarteXYZ

DayCarteXYZ is currently hiring! We are looking for translators capable of writing in Spanish or French, who are fluenct in math and technical language. If you aren’t a translator, that’s OK too; we are willing to hire anyone with a passion for math and learning.

DayCarteXYZ is a burgeoning math news site based in Cleavland, Ohio. It was founded by members Sam Parks and Michael Adams, and currently employs three author/editors, one proofreader and one designated researcher. Through DayCarteXYZ, we hope to spread knowledge and a love of niche and unusual new math developments.

We are dedicated to providing high-quality articles with a special focus on math news that hasn’t yet been reported. Any interesting new development we come across will be considered, no matter how inconsequential. If you think you know of a good candidate for one of our articles that we have not covered, we will gladly consider your suggestion.

Articles are written according to TypoStyle, a modified form of Associated Press Style. All content not created by employees of DayCarteXYZ will be credited to the copyright holder directly in the text.
